What's Included ?



Depending on your website design package chosen, you will receive all or some of these services.






Search Engine Optimisation

Your site will be optimised for higher search engine rankings.​ Our SEO expert (PhD degree in this area) will ensure the best results.









Australian Website Hosting

We host your websites on super fast cloud servers in Sydney. Enjoy our fully-managed cloud hosting with 99.99% uptime guarantee.









Google Analytics Setup​

We will configure your website with Google Analytics so that you can track all visitor activities and traffic sources.









WordFence Firewall

We will install Wordfence firewall – the world’s most popular plugin of WordPress for security. We will fine-tune the configurations for the best possible security.









Nightly Backups​

We will backup your entire site to our highly secured cloud storage on a daily basis. No dramas for any mistakes on content update or external threats.









Nightly Security Scans​

We will run a full security scan on your website every single day. It will capture any malware or infectious scripts. Instant actions will follow any detected issues.









SSL Certificate​

We will install an SSL certificate that makes your website HTTPS secure on all pages, which helps improve Google search rankings.









Caching

We will also setup multiple server-side caching systems that improve your site loading time dramatically.









All Social Media Links

We will link your website with all of your social medial channels to offer seamless experience of your brand to your site visitors.









Image Optimisation​

We will compress every image on your website without losing its crisp quality. This improves your website’s loading speed.









Speed Optimisation​

We have expertise in speed tuning of websites. We take a number of advanced techniques to ensure fast loading of your website.









Royalty-Free Stock Images

Stock images are expensive (typically $10 per image), we will happily include any stock images used on your website, with no additional charge.
